Frequently Asked Questions

What is ExpresaTec?

ExpresaTec is a place for students to give their input on any topic relating to school,
let it be to praise an individual or service area or, on the other hand, to freely complain
and let their concerns out with whatever they feel the need to (i.e: food service, health providers, etc.).

Why did you create this?

We need this application to promote freedom of speech without the fear of repercussions from
anyone, since users will be able to post anonymously to protect their identity.

What is the vision of this project?

Our vision is to create an impact in student life and well-being, in which the university can receive real feedback
and be able to react accordingly. Students care about their community and take advantage of our social
platform to make a change for the better in different aspects of their student life, such as socially and environmentally.

Who are the creators of this website?

We believe that everyone that participates in this project makes part of its creating.
Everyone in the Tec community can contribute to this project.
